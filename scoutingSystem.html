OVERVIEW OF GAME

In FIRST Power Up, teams are transported into an 8-bit video game. Each three-team alliance has three ways to help defeat the boss: Tipping the scale or the allianceâ€™s switch in their favor to earn points, exchanging power cubes for power ups (force, boost, and levitate) to gain a temporary advantage during the match, and climbing the scale tower to face the boss. The alliance with the highest score at the end of the match, including autonomous and teleoperated periods, defeats the boss and wins the game.
